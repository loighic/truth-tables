## truth tables, practice problems 4

These are optional problems, and you can work on them with others. 

Determine the truth values for each sentence, and then indicate whether the line is a good or a bad one by putting a `T` or an `F` under the turnstile (or a check or an X if those symbols are available). See section 11.4 in forallx for futher explanation. 


~~~{.TruthTable .Validity system="magnusSLPlus" options="turnstilemark nocounterexample" points="0"}
0.7 A <-> C, B & C :|-: A
~~~

~~~{.TruthTable .Validity system="magnusSLPlus" options="turnstilemark nocounterexample autoAtoms" points="0"}
0.8 A <-> ~C, B & ~C :|-: A v C
0.9 A <-> ~C, B & ~C :|-: A & C
0.10 (P & Q) -> R, ~Q :|-: P->R
0.11 A <-> ~C, ~C, B :|-: A & B
~~~
